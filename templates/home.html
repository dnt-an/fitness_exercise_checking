Bicep Curls

Curls work the biceps muscles at the front of the upper arm, and also the muscles of the lower arm. Doing the arm curl, you build strength and learn to use your arm muscles correctly, bracing with your core muscles.

Front Raise

The front raise strengthens primarily the shoulder but also works the upper chest muscles. It is an isolation exercise for shoulder flexion. This exercise will help you build strength and definition in the front and sides of your shoulder.

Let's do it!
Lateral Raise

The side lateral raise is an effective shoulder-strengthening movement designed to isolate the lateral head of the deltoid muscle. Performed regularly, this can help you develop stronger, broader shoulders. Lateral raises should be incorporated into upper-body strength training routines

Deep Squat

They don’t just help you achieve wonderful, toned legs; they promote body-wide muscle building by creating an anabolic (muscle building) environment in the body. They work up your quadriceps, hamstrings, calves, abdominal muscles, lower back and your butt too.
